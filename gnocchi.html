Gnocchi

The gnocchi is a very simple and filling dish composed of mostly potatoes and cheese.

For the preparations you will need:
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Potatoes

Now for the steps to be taken are listed below:

  1. Boil and mash the potatoes.
  2. Combine the ingredients, then knead into a ball.
  3. Shape the dough into "snakes."
  4. Cut the snakes into pieces.
  5. Boil and drain the gnocchi.

And your done!
