Pizza

Well i mean who doesnt know pizza. it's the most popular dish around the world tied with the burger.

For the preparations you will need:
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Mushroom

Now for the steps to be taken are listed below:

  1. Make a dough out of flour and the eggs
  2. Round the dough into a pie shape
  3. Once done add the chopped pepperoni and mushroom as toppings
  4. Lastly add your masterpiece to the oven for 30 mins at 200C

And your done!
